Fajitas

Home

Description

A classic Tex-Mex dish. Typically made with chicken, onions, bell peppers, and seasoning.

Ingredients:

  • Three chicken breasts
  • Red, green, and yellow bell peppers; one of each
  • One yellow onion
  • Fajita seasoning
  • Olive Oil>

Steps:

  1. Season chicken with dry rub/seasoning
  2. Put chicken into skillet on medium to medium-high heat with olive oil
  3. Slice half of onion and bell peppers into long strips
  4. Remove chicken after it is cooked, add vegetables
  5. Sautee vegetables for 4 to 5 minutes
  6. Slice chicken into strips
  7. Remove vegetables from skillet
  8. Serve as desired
